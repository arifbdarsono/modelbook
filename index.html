Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I book a model?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for standard bookings. For high-demand models or busy seasons (fashion weeks, holiday seasons), 1-2 months advance booking is advisable. For urgent requests, please contact us directly, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 or more days before the scheduled event receive a full refund minus a 10% administrative fee. Cancellations made 3-6 days prior receive a 50% refund. Cancellations with less than 72 hours notice are non-refundable. We understand that circumstances change, so please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

Can I request specific models for my event?

Yes, you can request specific models from our portfolio. Simply indicate your preference in the booking form or contact us directly. Availability depends on the model's schedule, so we recommend providing alternative options. If you're unsure which models would be best for your event, our team can help you select the most suitable talent based on your requirements.

Do you provide models for international events?

Yes, we provide models for international events. International bookings require additional planning for travel arrangements, visas, and accommodations. These costs are separate from the model booking fee and will be included in your custom quote. Please book international events at least 6-8 weeks in advance to ensure proper preparation.

What should I provide for the models during the event?

For events lasting more than 4 hours, you should provide appropriate breaks, refreshments, and meals. For photo shoots, a private changing area is required. For outdoor events, shelter from extreme weather conditions should be available. Our booking coordinator will discuss specific requirements based on your event type, duration, and location.

How are the pricing plans determined?

Our pricing is based on several factors including the model's experience level, event type, duration, location, and any special requirements. The prices listed on our website are standard rates. Custom quotes may vary based on specific needs. Volume discounts are available for multiple model bookings or long-term contracts.
